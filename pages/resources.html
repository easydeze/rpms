RPMS Resources

  • Volunteering & Shadowing Information Sheet - Check out this page for TMC hospital volunteering updates, and find virtual volunteering or shadowing opportunities!
  • Research Database - Check out this research database with over 800 labs across multiple fields of study at Rice and the Texas Medical Center across a variety of disciplines.
  • Pre-Med Guide - Check out the 2020 Rice Pre-Medical Guide, a 80+ page guide covering everything you could need to know from freshman year through the end of the application cycle and more!
  • Medical Workshops Folder - Check out our BOP Medical Workshops Folder to gain insight into different specialties and healthcare topics provided by doctors from the Texas Medical Center and beyond!
  • MCAT Book Drive - We offer a free MCAT book rental program. We do not require any personal or financial information, but we ask that you only apply if the cost of prep materials poses a financial hardship, as we currently have a limited amount of books available.
