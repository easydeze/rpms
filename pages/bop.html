Big Owl/Little Owl Program (BOP)

A mentorship program that connects small groups of Rice undergraduates with medical students, physicians, and residents. Meetings are usually held monthly in a casual setting, promoting discussion of medical issues and topics outside of academics such as lifestyle, aspirations, and the reality of being a medical student or physician. This semester, all meetings will occur over Zoom. Our meetings are from 7:00 p.m. - 8:30 p.m. The dates for our fall meetings are:

  • Meeting 1: September 27th
  • Meeting 2: October 20th
  • Meeting 3: November 17th

If you are interested in attending one of our small group meetings but did not fill out the initial interest form, please just reach out, and we can add you to our final meeting of the semester.

We also set up individual mentorships between undergrads and medical students. The date and time can be scheduled to the convenience of the mentor and mentee.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact the BOP Coordinators, Emily Wang (ew29@rice.edu) and Saagar Dhanjani (sd89@rice.edu), for more information.
